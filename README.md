

# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🧾 Project Overview

This project presents a **comprehensive Sales Dashboard** built using **Power BI**, designed to analyze business performance across multiple dimensions such as **sales, profit, customer behavior, regions, and product categories**.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly identify trends, monitor KPIs, and make **data-driven decisions**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall **sales and profitability**
* Understand **regional performance**
* Identify **top-performing categories and products**
* Evaluate **customer purchasing behavior**
* Track **monthly sales and profit trends**

---

## 📌 Key Metrics (KPIs)

* 💰 **Total Sales:** 1.6M
* 📈 **Total Profit:** 175K
* 📦 **Total Orders:** 22K

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Sales Analysis

* Sales breakdown by **Category** and **Sub-Category**
* Identification of **top-performing products**

### 🔹 Regional Insights

* Sales distribution across regions:

  * West (Highest)
  * East
  * Central
  * South (Lowest)

### 🔹 Customer Behavior

* Sales by **Segment** (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
* Payment mode analysis:

  * COD (42%)
  * Online (35%)
  * Cards (22%)

### 🔹 Time-Based Trends

* Monthly analysis of:

  * Sales trends
  * Profit trends
* Helps identify **seasonality patterns**

### 🔹 Shipping Analysis

* Sales by shipping modes:

  * Standard Class (Highest)
  * Second Class
  * First Class
  * Same Day

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* 📌 **West region** is the top contributor to overall sales (33%)
* 📌 **Cash on Delivery (COD)** is the most preferred payment method
* 📌 **Office Supplies** is the highest revenue-generating category
* 📌 **Phones and Chairs** are top-performing sub-categories
* 📌 Sales show **fluctuating monthly trends**, indicating seasonality
* 📌 Majority of customers prefer **standard shipping** over faster options

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* ✅ Increase marketing efforts in **South region** to boost sales
* ✅ Promote **digital payments** to reduce dependency on COD
* ✅ Focus on **high-performing products** like Phones & Chairs
* ✅ Optimize inventory based on **seasonal demand trends**
* ✅ Introduce incentives for **faster shipping adoption**
